About me

Hi, I'm River — an Indonesian who studied Business Management with Marketing at the University of Leeds. I'm a marketer at heart who believes in the power of a good story. However, as I've recently been drawn into the world of tech, software and engineering, I've been compelled to combine storytelling with technical skills to build things that are both functional and meaningful.

I'm competitive by nature — I've played football and rugby seriously, got into e-sports, and now I'm channelling that energy into running and fitness. That same drive compels me to do everything at a higher level, both in my professional and personal life.

This site is where I document that journey. Projects I've built, things I've learned, ideas I can't stop thinking about. I put it out there so it holds me accountable — and so whatever I learn, I can look back on it or retain it.
