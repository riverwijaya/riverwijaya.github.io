About me

Hi, I'm River an Indonesian who studied Business Management with Marketing at the University of Leeds. I'm a marketer at heart who believes in the power of a good story. However, as I've recently been drawn into the world of tech, software and engineering, I've been compelled to combine storytelling with technical skills to build things that are both functional and meaningful.

I'm competitive by nature I've played football and rugby seriously, got into e-sports, and now I'm channelling that energy into running and fitness. That same drive complies me do to everything I do at a higher level both in my professional and perosnal life.

This site is where I document that journey. Projects I've built, things I've learned, ideas I can't stop thinking about. I thought that if I put it out there, not only does it hold me accountable to learn and build at a deep enough level, but it also makes sure that whatever I learn I can look back to or retain.
