Built entirely from scratch during my internship at mGanik. The company had 42TB of unorganised digital assets. I built the system to fix that.

## Stack

- **Storage:** Synology NAS
- **DAM frontend:** ResourceSpace (open source)
- **OS:** Linux (debian and ubuntu)
- **Networking:** Port forwarding, HTTPS, wildcard SSL certificates
- **Access:** SSH container management, NFS4 volume mounting

## What I learned

You can learn almost anything if you break it into small enough pieces and aren't precious about looking stupid in the process.
