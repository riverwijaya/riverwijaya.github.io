The short version: I needed a Digital Asset Management system for mGanik's growing library of product images, brand assets, and marketing files. There was no budget for enterprise software. So I built one.

## What I used

- **Synology NAS** for storage
- **ResourceSpace** as the DAM front-end (open source)
- **Linux** (self-taught, from scratch) to configure everything
- A lot of Stack Overflow and sheer stubbornness

## What I learned

The biggest lesson wasn't technical. It was that you can learn almost anything if you break it into small enough pieces and aren't precious about looking stupid in the process.
