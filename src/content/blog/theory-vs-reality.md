Coming soon. The project that prompted this article — building a business-building framework for mGanik from scratch — is one of the most genuinely difficult things I've worked on.

Most frameworks assume you already know:

- Who your customer is
- What problem you're solving
- What market you're in

But what if you're a startup that's found product-market fit *accidentally*, and now needs to understand why it worked so you can do it again?

That's a very different problem. And STP doesn't help you much there.
